About Assistly

Assistly is an AI-powered chatbot assistant designed to help businesses provide instant, 24/7 customer support, capture leads automatically, and deliver personalized responses based on each business’s specific information. Its key features include instant replies to every customer inquiry, automatic lead capture and follow-up, personalized responses tailored to the business, and easy integration with websites, messaging platforms, and applications. The chatbot is user-friendly, quick to set up, and suitable for small and medium-sized businesses looking to enhance their customer support without hiring additional staff. Assistly’s pricing is simple and affordable, with a flat rate of 1000 INR per month. Beta users can gain early access and receive exclusive discounts. Overall, Assistly is designed to provide businesses with a professional, reliable, and intelligent assistant that improves communication, saves time, and helps turn inquiries into loyal customers.
